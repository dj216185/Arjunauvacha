About Our Community

Our Mission

To create a loving community where devotees come together to study the Bhagavad Gita, understand Krishna's divine teachings, and support each other's spiritual journey towards self-realization and devotion.

What We Study

We dive deep into the verses of Bhagavad Gita, exploring their meanings in Sanskrit, Hindi, and English. Our discussions help members apply these timeless teachings in modern life situations.

Our Values

Devotion (Bhakti), Knowledge (Jnana), Service (Seva), and Community (Sangha) form the foundation of our spiritual practice and interactions with fellow devotees.

Our Programs

Daily Gita Study Circle

Join our daily sessions where we read, discuss, and contemplate verses from the Bhagavad Gita. Perfect for beginners and advanced students alike.

Bhajan & Kirtan Sessions

Experience the joy of devotional singing with our weekly bhajan and kirtan programs. Let your heart sing in praise of Lord Krishna.

Meditation & Dharana

Learn ancient meditation techniques mentioned in the Gita. Develop concentration and inner peace through guided meditation sessions.

Community Service

Practice Karma Yoga through community service projects. Serve society while growing spiritually through selfless action.
